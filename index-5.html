How much is a taxi from Thame to Heathrow?
Our fixed price from Thame to Heathrow is £85 plus airport fees. The price is confirmed before your journey with no hidden extras.
Do you cover villages around Thame?
Yes. We cover 30+ villages across South Oxfordshire including Chinnor, Lewknor, Watlington, Haddenham, Tetsworth, Great Milton, Wheatley and many more.
Can I book a taxi by WhatsApp?
Yes — WhatsApp is our preferred booking channel. Message us on 07444 406443 for a quick quote or to pre-book your journey at any time.
Are you available for early morning airport runs?
Absolutely. We take pre-bookings for early morning airport transfers. We recommend booking the day before to guarantee your driver.
What happens if my flight is delayed?
We monitor your flight in real-time. If you land early or late, your driver will adjust accordingly — with no extra charge for delays.

Yes. 44Taxi is fully licensed by South Oxfordshire District Council (SODC). All drivers are DBS checked and all vehicles are safety inspected.
